**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Lucy Leai (Strategy) + health editor TBD + Sara Chen (CISO)
**Walks:** Mythos S2195 (Health v2 public-health long-form track) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** Health editor + medical fact-check standard.

## Why this exists

Health journalism is high-stakes for reader well-being. Mainstream press chases viral angles; GrimbaNews long-form public-health track delivers evidence-based explainer journalism with extra factuality scrutiny.

## Track topics

- Vaccine science + per-vaccine deep-dives.
- Antibiotic resistance: state of the threat.
- Mental health: per-condition explainers.
- Public-health vs individual-choice tensions (mandates, restrictions).
- Healthcare system comparison: per-country.
- Per-disease outbreaks: tracker + analysis.
- Reproductive health: legal landscape + science.
- Long COVID: research state.
- Pandemic preparedness: post-COVID lessons.

## Editorial standards (extra strict)

- Per-piece: peer-reviewed source citation required.
- Per-claim: NobuAI hallucination check (Wave ZZZZ).
- Per-piece: medical reviewer sign-off (gates on hiring).
- Per-piece: 7-day pre-publish review window.

## Per-piece structure

- 4000-6000 words.
- Glossary.
- "What we don't know" section (epistemic humility).
- "How to apply this" practical section.
- Per-living-piece update log.

## Cadence

- 1 long-form / month.
- Mid-month interim updates on living pieces.
