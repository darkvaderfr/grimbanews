## Why this exists

Lusophone Africa = Angola, Mozambique, Cabo Verde, Guiné-Bissau, São Tomé. PT-language detector already covers per `GrimbaLanguageDetector`. Source seeding is operator-side editorial pickup.

## Tier-1 sources per country

- **Angola:** Jornal de Angola (state), Novo Jornal (private/independent), Folha 8 (private/critical).
- **Mozambique:** Notícias (state), O País (private), Verdade (private/digital).
- **Cabo Verde:** A Nação (private), Inforpress (state agency).
- **Guiné-Bissau:** RFI Português (FR national broadcaster, Guiné-Bissau bureau), Bissaudigital.
- **São Tomé:** Jornal Tropical (private).

## Editorial guardrails

- State vs private balance per country.
- Per-country political-context sensitivity (Angola post-MPLA reform, Mozambique post-Cabo Delgado conflict, etc.).
- Per-country counsel review for license + sanctions compliance.
