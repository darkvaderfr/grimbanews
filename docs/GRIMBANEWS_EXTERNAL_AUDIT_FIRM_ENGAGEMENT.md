# GrimbaNews — External Audit Firm Engagement Plan

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Sara Chen (CISO) + Ray Dalio (CFO) + Michael O'Connor (Legal)
**Walks:** Mythos S1892 (external-audit firm engagement) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** Firm shortlist + selection (SUB-55 sister, S1891).

## Why this exists

Once a firm is selected from the S1891 shortlist, the engagement is a multi-month process with legal, financial, technical, and operational moving parts. This doc walks the engagement from selection through signed contract through Year-1 kickoff.

## Per-phase engagement workflow

### Phase 1 — Selection (post-RFP)

- Per-finalist scoring matrix completed by Sara Chen + Ray Dalio.
- Per-finalist reference checks (minimum two clients of comparable size + industry).
- Per-finalist proposal-clarification call.
- CEO sign-off on the chosen firm.

### Phase 2 — Engagement letter + MSA

Per-firm contract review by Michael O'Connor with attention to:
- Per-engagement scope statement (must match S1811 SOC 2 scope decision).
- Per-engagement deliverable list (Readiness Assessment? Type I? Type II? Bridge letter?).
- Per-engagement fee schedule (per-year + scope-change pricing + travel).
- Per-engagement timing (kickoff, mid-point, draft report, final report).
- Per-engagement personnel (partner, manager, senior, staff — request CVs).
- Per-engagement independence representations (auditor cannot also be a consultant on the controls).
- Per-engagement work-paper retention + access rules.
- Per-engagement subsequent-period coverage if Year-2 also engaged.
- Per-engagement termination clauses (mutual + for cause).

### Phase 3 — Kickoff

- Per-engagement kickoff meeting (CISO + auditor partner + engagement manager).
- Per-engagement control walkthrough scheduled.
- Per-engagement evidence-request list received.
- Per-engagement single-point-of-contact (SPOC) designated on our side (Sara Chen).
- Per-engagement secure evidence-sharing channel agreed (typically auditor's portal).

### Phase 4 — Fieldwork support

- Per-engagement weekly status meetings during fieldwork.
- Per-engagement evidence requests routed through SPOC.
- Per-engagement interviews scheduled efficiently (batch by topic).
- Per-engagement clarifying-question turnaround target: 2 business days.

### Phase 5 — Draft report + management response

- Per-draft-report review by CISO + CEO + Legal.
- Per-finding (if any) management response drafted in coordination with auditor.
- Per-bridge-letter if needed for the gap to a subsequent period.

### Phase 6 — Final report + customer distribution

- Per-final-report secured storage + version control.
- Per-final-report distribution policy (NDA-gated; via trust center request).
- Per-final-report annual refresh scheduling for Year 2 (Phase 1 starts ~Q3 of Year 1).

## Per-engagement governance

Audit Committee briefed at:
- Selection decision (Phase 1 close).
- Engagement letter signed (Phase 2 close).
- Kickoff (Phase 3 start).
- Draft report (Phase 5).
- Final report (Phase 6).
