## SLA per-partner reviewed quarterly

- **Uptime:** % of attempted polls successful (target ≥ 95%).
- **Freshness:** median lag publisher-publish → GrimbaNews ingest (target < 30 min).
- **Per-cluster contribution stability:** monthly variance (target < ±20%).
- **Reader-feedback negative rate:** % negative feedback on partner content (target < 5%).
- **Brand-safety incidents:** count of flagged clusters needing operator action (target = 0).
- **Editorial-style adherence:** per-partner review on house-style match (subjective; target = pass).

## Review template

Quarterly per-partner review meeting:
- 30 min per-partner discussion
- Per-metric red/yellow/green status
- Action items + remediation timeline
- Renewal recommendation

## Renewal/termination decision tree

- All green: auto-renew per contract.
- 1-2 yellow: renew with remediation plan.
- 3+ yellow or any red: revise contract OR initiate termination (Wave AAMM).
