# GrimbaNews — Investigations Awards Submission Cadence

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Lucy Leai (Strategy) + Vader
**Walks:** Mythos S2214 (Long-form investigations awards-submission cadence) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** Operator-side recognition strategy.

## Why this exists

Journalism awards validate editorial work + drive reader acquisition + improve hiring. GrimbaNews investigations track requires deliberate awards-submission cadence.

## Eligible awards (per locale)

### Francophone
- **Albert-Londres** (FR, prestigious, annual)
- **Prix Bayeux-Calvados des correspondants de guerre** (FR, war/foreign)
- **Prix de la presse étrangère** (FR)
- **Prix Camille Desmoulins** (FR investigative)
- **CIJ scoop d'or** (FR)

### European
- **European Press Prize** (multi-category, annual)
- **Lorenzo Natali Media Prize** (EU development reporting)
- **European Sustainability Reporting Award**

### Anglophone (when EN content)
- **Pulitzer** (US, multi-category)
- **Polk Award** (US)
- **Online Journalism Award** (US, ONA)
- **British Press Awards** (UK)
- **Orwell Prize** (UK political writing)

### Global
- **Reuters Institute fellowships** (research recognition)
- **OCCRP awards** (organized-crime/corruption)
- **Online News Association awards**

## Per-investigation submission timing

- Per-investigation eligible-for-awards check by Lucy.
- Per-submission window typically 6-12 months post-publication.
- Per-investigation outcome tracked + publicized.

## Per-award follow-up

- Each submission: per-jury feedback collected (when shared).
- Per-loss: editorial takeaway logged.
- Per-win: full announcement campaign.
