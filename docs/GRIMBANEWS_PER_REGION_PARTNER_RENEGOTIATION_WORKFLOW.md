# GrimbaNews — Per-Region Partner Renegotiation Workflow

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Lucy Leai (Strategy) + counsel + Ray Dalio (CFO)
**Walks:** Mythos S1669 (per-region partner renegotiation workflow) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** First contracted-partner reaching renewal window.

## Why this exists

Partnership agreements have term (per `docs/GRIMBANEWS_SYNDICATION_AGREEMENT_TEMPLATE.md` Wave LLL — 12 months auto-renew). Renegotiation window opens 60 days before term-end. Workflow:

1. 60 days pre-term: ops alert via cockpit + email to Lucy.
2. 45 days pre: Lucy + per-region editor + partner editor review per-partner metrics.
3. 30 days pre: term-renewal letter sent OR amendment proposed.
4. 15 days pre: counsel review of any amendments.
5. Term-end: signed renewal OR termination per Wave AAMM workflow.

## Metrics reviewed

- Partner content volume (vs target)
- Partner reader engagement (clicks, shares, dwell time)
- Partner attribution-source health (uptime)
- Mutual brand-safety incidents (per Wave AAMM banning)
- Revenue share (if applicable per partnership-program plan)
- Editorial-style adherence (per Wave LLL editorial-style guide)

## Negotiation toolkit

Per-partner negotiation room:
- Volume tier adjustment (more / less content)
- Attribution-prominence adjustment
- Revenue-share term adjustment
- Per-clause amendment
