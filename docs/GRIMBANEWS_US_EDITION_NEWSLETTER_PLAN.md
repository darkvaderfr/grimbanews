## Why this exists

US is the largest English-language audience. Per-region US newsletter delivers US-relevant coverage with appropriate political-framing nuance distinct from UK/global EN editions.

## Newsletter design

- Daily 07:00 ET (12:00 UTC).
- US-centric headline cluster + 3 dossiers.
- US Middle Ground signal (where US left + right converge).
- US Blindspots (often partisan-split coverage).
- Per-US-state breakdown when relevant.

## US-source roster

- AP (Associated Press) — wire
- Reuters US section — wire
- NYT, WaPo, WSJ — top tier
- AP regional members per state
- The Hill, Politico, Axios — DC politics
- Per-state public broadcasters (KQED, WBUR, etc.)
- AllSides — bias-audit peer
- Ground News — peer

## Editorial guardrails

- US election cycle: per Wave AAGG election period guardrails.
- US gun-control / abortion / immigration: extra-strict source-balance (each side editorially loud).
- Per-state coverage diversity (avoid coastal-bias).
