## Event calendar

- **France:** Cannes Film Festival, Festival d'Avignon, Salon du Livre, Hellfest, Fête de la Musique, Rencontres d'Arles, FIAC.
- **Quebec:** Festival d'été de Québec, Just for Laughs (Montreal), Quebec City Summer Festival.
- **Belgium:** Brussels Film Festival, Fête de la Musique.
- **Switzerland:** Montreux Jazz, Locarno Film Festival.
- **Africa francophone:** FESPACO (Burkina Faso film fest), Marché des arts du spectacle d'Abidjan, Salon international du livre et de la presse de Genève.
- **Caribbean:** Festival international de jazz et de musique en Guadeloupe.

## Per-event coverage cadence

- T-1 month: preview piece + tickets/access info.
- T-1 week: pre-event briefing.
- T-0 to T+festival days: daily coverage cluster.
- T+1 week: retrospective + best-of.
- T+1 month: lasting-impact analysis.

## Per-event partner outreach

Each major event has a press office. Per-event partner agreement for accreditation + early-access materials.
