# GrimbaNews — Exit / Expansion Criteria

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Vader + Lucy Leai (Strategy) + Ray Dalio (CFO)
**Walks:** Mythos S2233 (exit / expansion criteria) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** Operator-side strategic decision.

## Why this exists

5-year + maturity phase decision: continue + expand, hand-off, sunset, OR sell to peer. Pre-defined criteria reduces decision-fatigue + emotional bias.

## Expansion criteria (continue)

- Annual revenue > €500K
- Annual reader growth > 25%
- Per-region subscription tier breakeven
- Editorial team stable + recruiting healthy
- Personal energy + interest from founders

## Hand-off criteria (succession)

- Annual revenue > €1M (sustainable)
- Operational team capable of running without founders
- Successor identified
- 12-month transition planned

## Sunset criteria (close gracefully)

- Annual revenue < €100K + declining
- Subscriber growth flat for 3 years
- Editorial team turnover > 50%
- Per-locale legal risk > insurance cap
- 12-month wind-down with reader notice

## Sale criteria (peer acquisition)

- Strategic peer interested + offer > 3-year-revenue NPV
- Independence preserved post-sale (NYT-style)
- Per-acquisition terms preserve methodology + editorial independence

## Per-criteria annual review

- Per-criteria Vader + Lucy + Ray review.
- Per-decision: founders' call only.
