## Why this exists

Africa edition needs a named, public-facing curator — a face for the audience, accountable for editorial decisions, building trust with African readers.

- Senior editorial experience covering Africa francophone + anglophone.
- 5+ years in African press or pan-African outlet.
- Based in West / Central / North / East Africa (proximity).
- French fluent; English working level; Arabic / Swahili bonus.
- Per-region travel willingness (annual editor meetings).

- Per-day editorial review of African cluster mix.
- Per-week Africa edition newsletter curation.
- Per-month Africa transparency report.
- Per-quarter Africa Trust Report (Wave AABB pattern, Africa-scoped).
- Per-major-event coverage (elections, crises, summits).
- Public-facing speaking engagements (industry conferences).
