## Why this exists

In France, BnF (Bibliothèque nationale de France) operates a legal deposit (dépôt légal) for press publications. Above a circulation threshold, registration is mandatory. Quebec's BAnQ has similar policy for Quebec-based publishers.

## Per-jurisdiction obligations

### France (BnF)
- Above 500 readers / day for online press: dépôt légal required.
- GrimbaNews growth target crosses threshold within Year 2.
- Per-quarter web-archive submission via BnF SPAR system.
- Per-publication metadata required: ISSN, publisher, deposit timestamp.

### Quebec (BAnQ)
- Required for Quebec-based publishers regardless of size.
- If GrimbaNews Quebec edition launches, registration required.

### Other jurisdictions (when local edition launches)
- Belgium: KBR.
- Switzerland: Swiss National Library.
- Germany: DNB.
- Per-country counsel review.

## Operational impact

- Per-publication deposit cadence: monthly or per-publication.
- Metadata: ISSN application (if not already held).
- Per-jurisdiction renewal cadence.
