## Why this exists

COPs (Conference of Parties — climate-treaty summits) are annual editorial flagship events. Mainstream coverage spikes during the 2-week summit then disappears. GrimbaNews can sustain pre-summit + summit + post-summit + 12-month-followup coverage for a fuller arc.

## Coverage arc (per COP, ~14 months)

- **T-12 months:** Pre-summit framing — what was agreed last COP, what's contested.
- **T-6 months:** Negotiating-track previews — per-track briefings.
- **T-3 months:** Pre-summit briefings — what to watch.
- **T-1 month:** Daily pre-summit dossier rotation.
- **Summit (2 weeks):** Daily live-update + daily cluster.
- **T+1 month:** Settled-narrative cluster + accountability tracker setup.
- **T+6 months:** Mid-year reality-check — what's implemented vs promised.
- **T+12 months:** Pre-next-COP framing.

## Per-COP dossier surface

- `/dossiers/cop-{N}/` permanent landing.
- Per-track sub-dossiers: mitigation, adaptation, finance, loss-damage.
- Per-country positions tracker.
- Per-commitment progress chart.

## Editorial cadence

- Pre-summit: 2 articles/week.
- Summit: 4+ articles/day.
- Post-summit: weekly settled-narrative updates.
- 12-month followup: monthly accountability check-ins.
