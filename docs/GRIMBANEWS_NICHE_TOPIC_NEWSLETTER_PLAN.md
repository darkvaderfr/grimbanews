**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Lucy Leai (Strategy) + per-topic editor

**Gating dependency:** Newsletter v2 + v2 taxonomy sub-buckets live.

## Why this exists

Beyond per-topic newsletters (Wave UUUU), niche sub-buckets warrant their own narrow newsletters for power users (e.g. "Quantum Computing weekly," "FESPACO coverage," "Long COVID research updates").

## Per-niche newsletter examples

- "Climate science weekly" (sub-bucket of Climat)
- "Quantum + cryptography quarterly" (sub-bucket of Sciences fondamentales)
- "Per-COP coverage daily during summit windows" (per-event newsletter)
- "Per-IPCC-report briefing" (per-event newsletter)
- "Francophone diaspora cultural roundup" (sub-bucket of Culture)

## Reader signup

- /sujets/{sub-bucket} page CTA
- /account/newsletter granular toggles
- Per-niche editor's introduction

## Cadence

Per-niche: weekly, monthly, or per-event depending on volume.
