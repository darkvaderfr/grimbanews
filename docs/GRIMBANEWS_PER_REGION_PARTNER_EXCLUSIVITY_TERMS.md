# GrimbaNews — Per-Region Partner Exclusivity Terms

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Lucy Leai (Strategy) + counsel + Ray Dalio (CFO)
**Walks:** Mythos S1685 (per-region partner exclusivity terms) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** First partner negotiating exclusivity clause.

## Why this exists

Some partners want exclusivity in their region (no competing newsroom partnership in same country). Need clear policy on how this works without locking out future opportunities.

## Tiers of exclusivity

- **None:** Default. GrimbaNews can partner with multiple newsrooms in same region.
- **Soft:** No new partnership within ±15% audience overlap for 6 months.
- **Hard:** No new partnership in same country/segment for 12 months.
- **Strategic:** Hard + first-right-of-refusal on adjacent regions.

## Cost differential

- Tier-1 partnerships (Soft+): standard fees.
- Hard exclusivity: +30% partnership fee (per Ray Dalio unit economics).
- Strategic exclusivity: negotiated case-by-case.

## Termination clause

- Exclusivity lapses if partner misses material SLA targets (per Wave AAOO quarterly review).
- 90-day notice for exclusivity termination.
