# GrimbaNews — ISO 27001 Internal Audit Plan

**Status:** plan v0

**Gating dependency:** S1881 (internal audit cadence band).

## Annual internal audit scope

Per ISO 27001 9.2 requirements: per-year audit of ISMS by independent auditor (not implementer of controls).

## Per-audit schedule

- Q1: Information security policies + organization (A.5 + A.6).
- Q2: People controls + access management (A.6 + A.8).
- Q3: Technology controls + supplier relationships (A.7 + A.8).
- Q4: Incident management + business continuity + compliance (A.5 + A.7).

## Per-audit deliverable

Per-quarter internal-audit report:
- Per-control assessed.
- Per-finding (non-conformity vs observation).
- Per-finding owner + remediation timeline.
- Per-report management review per Wave SUB-40 sister.

## Internal-audit independence (per Wave LLL `docs/GRIMBANEWS_INTERNAL_AUDIT_CHARTER.md`)

- Per-audit by person not responsible for controls being audited.
- Per-audit reports directly to Vader.
- Per-audit budget protected from operational management.
