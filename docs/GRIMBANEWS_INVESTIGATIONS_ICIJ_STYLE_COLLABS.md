# GrimbaNews — Investigations ICIJ-Style Cross-Outlet Collaborations

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Lucy Leai (Strategy) + investigative reporter (when hired) + peer-outlet contacts
**Walks:** Mythos S2217 (Long-form investigations collaborative with peer outlets) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** First investigation establishes credibility for peer invitations.

## Why this exists

International Consortium of Investigative Journalists (ICIJ) model — outlets collaborate on large cross-border investigations (Panama Papers, Pandora Papers). GrimbaNews participation in 1-2 collabs/year multiplies impact.

## Collab models

- **Receiving collaborator:** GrimbaNews joins per-outlet-led collab + contributes per-locale angle.
- **Leading collaborator:** GrimbaNews leads cross-outlet collab (later stage; high credibility required).
- **Per-collab editor partnership:** Iboga Ventures editorial peer-of-peer agreement.

## Per-collab structure

- Pre-collab: MOU between participating outlets.
- Shared SecureDrop or equivalent infra.
- Per-outlet exclusive angle preserved.
- Coordinated publish-date.
- Per-outlet attribution + cross-link.
- Per-collab counsel review (multi-jurisdictional risk).

## Membership pursuit

- ICIJ membership requires multi-year investigative record.
- GPI (Global Investigative Journalism Network) more accessible early.
- OCCRP (Organized Crime and Corruption Reporting Project) partnership possible.
