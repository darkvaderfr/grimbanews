**Walks:** Mythos S1329 (Per-partner case study) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** At least one partnership shipped + per-partner analytics shipped (S1325) + 6 months of operating data + partner consent

## Why this exists

S1329 is GrimbaNews' partner-validated proof for new-partner outbound. Case studies move BD conversations from "this product exists" to "this partner found X value". Without them, every new partner conversation re-litigates value.

## Today's surrogate

- **Partnership program launch playbook** (`docs/GRIMBANEWS_PARTNERSHIP_PROGRAM_LAUNCH_PLAYBOOK.md`) — points toward case studies as outcome metric.
- **Methodology page** — broader proof of editorial discipline.

## Case study format

### Section 1 — partner profile

- Partner name + URL.
- Industry / vertical.
- Country.
- Logo (with permission).

### Section 2 — challenge

- What problem motivated partnership.
- Pre-GrimbaNews approach.
- Limitations observed.

### Section 3 — solution (GrimbaNews offering)

- Which tier (content-share / API / OEM whitelabel).
- Specific integration approach.
- Timeline from contract to value.

### Section 4 — outcomes (numbers from S1325 analytics)

- Posts ingested / published.
- Reader reach.
- Engagement metrics.
- Cluster contribution.
- MG-tag appearances.

### Section 5 — partner quote

- 2-3 sentences from partner contact.
- Approved verbatim.

### Section 6 — what's next

- Tier upgrade / expansion plans.
- New use cases identified.

## Criteria for case-study eligibility

- Partnership ≥ 6 months active.
- Concrete measurable outcomes (S1325 data).
- Partner consent to public case study.
- No competitive-sensitivity blocker.

## Publishing cadence

- 1 case study per quarter (Henry Walker's content calendar).
- Mix of tiers (content-share, API, OEM) for breadth.
- Mix of regions (FR, EN, ES, PT-BR markets).

## Distribution

- Hosted at `/case-studies/{partner-slug}`.
- Featured on `/partnerships` landing page.
- Press blurb at launch (Gary + Olivia).
- Sales asset library (Victor + Chris Johnson).
- LinkedIn Pulse (Maria Lopez via Lucy account).
