**Gating dependency:** Operator-side governance pickup.

## Per ISO 27001 9.3 requirements

Management review at planned intervals to ensure ISMS effectiveness.

## Per-quarter management review agenda

1. Status of actions from previous review.
2. Changes in external/internal issues relevant to ISMS.
3. Information security performance (per-control trends + per-incident summary).
4. Per-internal-audit findings (Wave SUB-40).
5. Per-customer feedback on security posture.
6. Resource adequacy for ISMS.
7. Opportunities for continual improvement.

## Per-review deliverable

Per-quarter Management Review minutes:
- Per-agenda-item notes.
- Per-decision rationale.
- Per-action-item owner + due date.
- Archived in ISMS evidence vault per Wave SUB-35.

## Per-emergency review

Per-incident with material ISMS impact: ad-hoc management review within 30 days.
