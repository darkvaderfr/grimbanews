# GrimbaNews — Investigations Pricing Decision (Premium vs Free)

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Ray Dalio (CFO) + Lucy Leai (Strategy) + Vader
**Walks:** Mythos S2218 (Long-form investigations pricing decision) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** Stripe subscription tier (S1211 partial).

## Why this exists

Investigations cost $5-20K each. Pricing decision: free-for-all (maximize impact, fund via subscriptions) vs premium-tier paywall (direct ROI) vs hybrid.

## Options

### Option A: Fully free
- Investigations: free + indexed + open republication
- Funded via subscription tier + advertising
- Impact-maximized; reach-maximized
- ROI indirect (subscriber-acquisition driver)

### Option B: Premium-tier only
- Investigations: premium subscriber-only first 30 days
- After 30 days: free
- Direct ROI to subscription
- Reach narrower in first 30 days

### Option C: Hybrid
- Investigations: free TLDR + summary; full investigation + companion data premium-only
- Free-tier reads summary; pays to read full
- Compromise between reach + ROI

## Recommendation

Option A for first 3 investigations (build credibility). Re-evaluate based on subscriber-acquisition data.

## Per-option metrics

- Reach: total readers + share rate
- Direct ROI: subscriptions attributable
- Indirect ROI: brand-uplift + awards
- Impact: outcome tracking (per Wave SUB-12)
