# GrimbaNews — Investigations Counsel Review Workflow

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Lucy Leai (Strategy) + retained counsel + Vader (sign-off)
**Walks:** Mythos S2208 (Long-form investigations counsel review) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** Retained press counsel onboarded.

## Why this exists

Investigative pieces carry defamation + privacy + access-to-info risk. Pre-publish counsel review prevents costly litigation.

## Per-investigation counsel timeline

- T-30 days pre-publish: counsel briefed on scope + key claims
- T-14: counsel reviews draft + per-claim verification (per Wave SUB-9 fact-check)
- T-7: counsel sign-off on revised draft
- T-3: final counsel clearance
- T-0: publish

## Per-claim risk levels

- **Green:** Factual, well-sourced, public-record — publish as written.
- **Yellow:** Single-source or interpretive — counsel suggests wording.
- **Red:** Defamation-risk or private-figure exposure — counsel + Vader sign-off required.

## Per-piece sign-off record

Counsel signs off in writing per-piece. Archived alongside the investigation.
