# GrimbaNews — Investigations Press-Release Distribution to Peer Outlets

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Lucy Leai (Strategy) + per-investigation comms
**Walks:** Mythos S2213 (Long-form investigations press-release distribution) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** Operator-side comms; peer-outlet contact roster.

## Why this exists

When GrimbaNews publishes an original investigation, peer outlets (Le Monde, Mediapart, AFP, Reuters) often pick it up. Proactive press-release distribution amplifies reach + cements GrimbaNews's investigative credibility.

## Per-investigation press kit

- 250-word executive summary
- Top-3 key findings
- Suggested headline + lede
- Pull-quotes available for republication
- Per-outlet pre-publish embargo (24h) when offered
- Per-outlet exclusive material when negotiable

## Distribution channels

- AFP (newswire) — automatic + paid distribution.
- Peer francophone press (Le Monde, Libération, Mediapart, La Croix, etc.).
- Anglophone press (Reuters, AP, Guardian) for translated brief.
- Per-region partners (Wave LLL partnership program members).

## Embargo policy

- Per-investigation 24h embargo offered to top peer outlets.
- Embargo-respect tracked; per-outlet embargo violations flagged.
- Post-embargo: open republication with attribution.

## Per-investigation comms calendar

T-7: outreach to peer outlets.
T-3: embargo offer.
T-1: pre-publish briefing call with embargoed outlets.
T-0: simultaneous publish + open.
