## Why this exists

French-speaking diaspora (North America, Africa, Caribbean) produces vibrant cultural output that mainstream Hexagone press underserves. GrimbaNews coverage validates these audiences + sources unique stories.

## Diaspora cultural surfaces to cover

- **Francophone Canada:** Montreal music scene (Cirque, indie, hip-hop), Quebec literature (Dany Laferrière, Heather O'Neill in translation).
- **US francophone:** Louisiana Creole revival, Acadian music.
- **Caribbean francophone:** Patrick Chamoiseau, zouk, kompa, Carnaval culture.
- **African francophone diaspora:** Afrobeats crossover, Souleymane Diamanka, Tiken Jah Fakoly.
- **Maghreb diaspora:** rai, hip-hop, Algerian-French film.

## Per-diaspora source roster

- **Le Devoir** (Montreal) — Quebec.
- **Africalia / Africultures** — pan-African.
- **PAN African Music** — pan-African online music journalism.
- **Mediapart-international section** — diaspora-aware coverage.
- **Jeune Afrique** — francophone Africa + diaspora.

## Editorial cadence

- Weekly per-diaspora cluster (rotating focus).
- Per-event coverage (FESPACO, JdM Cinq Continents, francophone book fairs).
- Per-artist profile commitment monthly.
