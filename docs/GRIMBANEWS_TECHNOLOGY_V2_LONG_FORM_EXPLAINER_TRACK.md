## Why this exists

Tech news flows in short bursts. Long-form explainers ("How LLMs actually work," "What end-to-end encryption means for your messages," "Why this AI safety debate matters") deliver lasting value + reader trust.

## Per-explainer structure

- 5000+ words.
- Visual diagrams + interactive elements.
- Glossary embedded.
- Reading-time estimate.
- "Update log" appended as tech evolves (per Wave AAEE narrative phase concept).

## Topic queue

- LLMs + how they actually work
- End-to-end encryption + Signal vs WhatsApp
- AI safety: alignment + capabilities + governance
- Web3 + crypto: what's real vs hype
- Quantum computing: state of the art + threat to crypto
- Open source vs proprietary AI
- Content moderation + free speech tensions
- Per-platform algorithmic transparency
- Privacy regulation: GDPR vs CCPA vs LGPD comparison
- Cybersecurity primer: zero days + bug bounties + responsible disclosure

## Cadence

- 1 long-form / month (steady state).
- Mid-month update on living explainers.
- Yearly "tech state of the art" roll-up.
