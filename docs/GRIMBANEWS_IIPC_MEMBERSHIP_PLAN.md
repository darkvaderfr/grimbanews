**Status:** plan v0

## Why this exists

IIPC (International Internet Preservation Consortium) is a coalition of national libraries + major archives. Membership gives GrimbaNews access to:
- WARC archival standard expertise
- Cross-archive interoperability
- Multi-national mirror network
- Per-event coordinated archival (elections, crises)

## Membership tiers

- **Full member:** national archives, NARA-tier.
- **Associate member:** publisher / research institution. Best fit for GrimbaNews.
- **Annual fee:** ~€2K associate tier.

## Benefits

- WARC tooling support.
- Cross-archive peering (per-mirror).
- Per-event archival coordination.
- Annual conference participation (networking + best practices).

## Application process

- Per-organization application form.
- Per-member endorsement (need 2 endorsers from current members; gates on first-year networking).
- Per-quarter review by IIPC board.
