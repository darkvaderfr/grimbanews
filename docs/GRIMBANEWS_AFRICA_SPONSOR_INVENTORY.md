## Why this exists

Beyond programmatic ads, GrimbaNews Africa edition offers per-section sponsorship slots: per-region newsletter sponsor, per-topic sponsor, per-investigation sponsor.

## Inventory tiers

- **Per-region newsletter sponsor:** monthly slot in regional digest. €500-2000/mo.
- **Per-topic sponsor:** per-topic page banner + per-cluster footer. €1000-3000/mo.
- **Per-investigation sponsor:** investigation page banner + first-pull-quote sponsor. €5000-15000/piece.

## Per-sponsor editorial guardrails

- No editorial influence permitted.
- Per-sponsor brand-safety check (per Wave AAQQ).
- Per-sponsor counsel review for license + sanctions.
- Per-sponsor "Sponsored content" badge in compliance with FTC + ARPP + per-country ad-disclosure law.

## Sales motion

- Africa curator + Lucy + Ray closes top-tier sponsors.
- Per-sponsor 6-12 month contracts.
- Per-sponsor monthly performance review.
