## Why this exists

DACH (DE + AT + CH) is GrimbaNews's first non-Romance launch market. German-language editorial cadence differs materially from FR: register, vocab, formality, news-cycle rhythm.

## Per-country cadence

- **Germany:** Daily morning (07:00 UTC) + evening (18:00 UTC) cycles. Bundestags-Sitzung tracking on Wednesdays.
- **Austria:** Daily; Nationalrat tracking.
- **Switzerland:** Weekly Bundesversammlung tracking + per-canton coverage variability.

## Tier-1 sources per country

- **Germany:** Süddeutsche Zeitung (center-left), Die Welt (center-right), FAZ (center-right), Spiegel (center-left), TAZ (left), Bild (right/tabloid), Tagesspiegel (center), Handelsblatt (center-right/business).
- **Austria:** Der Standard (center-left), Die Presse (center-right), Kurier (center), Salzburger Nachrichten (center-right).
- **Switzerland:** Neue Zürcher Zeitung (center-right, FR pickup also), Tages-Anzeiger (center), Le Temps (FR), Blick (center).

## Editorial guardrails

- Compound-noun handling (DE-specific) in summaries.
- Formal Sie register (per Wave WWW DE catalog).
- AfD coverage: extra source-balance scrutiny per Wave AAEE election guardrails.
- Per-Land coverage diversity (not just Berlin / München).
