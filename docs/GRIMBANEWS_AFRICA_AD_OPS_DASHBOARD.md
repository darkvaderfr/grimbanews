# GrimbaNews — Africa Ad-Ops Dashboard

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Lucy Leai (Strategy) + Africa curator (when hired) + Ray Dalio (CFO)
**Walks:** Mythos S1756 (Africa ad-ops dashboard) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** S1755 (Africa-region ad inventory live).

## Why this exists

Africa edition needs region-specific ad inventory tracking + reporting. Per-Africa advertisers (Africa-focused brands, telecom operators, fintech) have distinct sales cycle from Europe.

## Dashboard

`/admin/grimba/africa/ads`:
- Per-advertiser impressions + clicks per Africa region.
- Per-country impression density.
- Per-campaign performance vs target.
- Per-month ad-revenue trend.
- Per-source-of-traffic per ad (organic search vs newsletter vs direct).

## Per-region pricing tiers

- Tier-1 markets (South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya, Egypt): standard rates.
- Tier-2 markets (Senegal, Ghana, Côte d'Ivoire, Morocco): -30% to attract early advertisers.
- Tier-3 markets (other): negotiated per campaign.

## Per-advertiser monthly export

CSV + PDF emailed to each Africa advertiser monthly.
