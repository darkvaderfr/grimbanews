# GrimbaNews — Investigations Companion Podcast / Video

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Lucy Leai (Strategy) + investigative reporter + audio/video producer (when contracted)
**Walks:** Mythos S2212 (Long-form investigations companion podcast/video) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** Podcast pipeline (Wave AABB) + video summary integration (Wave AABB).

## Why this exists

Investigations resonate harder with audio + video companions. 30-min podcast or 5-min explainer-video adapted per investigation.

## v1 design

For each major investigation:
- **Podcast (30-45 min):** investigator-narrated walkthrough + interview with key sources (when consent permits).
- **Video (5-7 min):** explainer-style with data viz + reporter on-camera.

## Production cadence

- T-14: script-write + storyboarding.
- T-7: recording.
- T-3: edit + counsel review.
- T-0 (publish day): companion drops alongside text article.

## Distribution

- Podcast: Apple Podcasts, Spotify, YouTube (per Wave AABB podcast plan).
- Video: YouTube, Vimeo, embedded on article page.

## Per-companion accessibility

- Podcast: full transcript published.
- Video: subtitles in FR + EN minimum.
