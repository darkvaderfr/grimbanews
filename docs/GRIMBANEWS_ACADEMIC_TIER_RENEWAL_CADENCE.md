# GrimbaNews — Academic-Tier Renewal Cadence

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Lucy Leai (Strategy) + Liam Smith (PM)
**Walks:** Mythos S1699 (academic-tier renewal cadence) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** S1693 academic-tier license + verification process.

## Why this exists

Academic credentials change (graduation, role transitions). Per-12-month renewal ensures roster stays accurate + license compliance maintained.

## Renewal workflow

- T-30 days pre-expiry: automated email + dashboard reminder.
- T-7 days: second reminder.
- T-0 expiry: read-only access (no new calls until renewed).
- T+30 days: account paused with re-activation flow.
- T+90 days: account archived (re-application required).

## Re-verification

- Re-confirm institution email validity.
- Re-confirm research role (student / researcher / professor).
- Optional: per-grant linkage refresh.

## Per-renewal audit

- Per-12-month: Lucy reviews edge cases (former-student transitions, institution changes).
- Per-renewal: usage trend review (low-usage may warrant tier-downgrade conversation).
