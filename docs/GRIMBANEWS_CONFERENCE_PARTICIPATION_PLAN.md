# GrimbaNews — Conference Participation Plan

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Lucy Leai (Strategy) + Henry Walker (Editorial) + Maria Lopez (Community)
**Walks:** Mythos S2147 (Médias en Seine / academic-conference participation) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** operator-side travel budget + speaker bench + ≥ 1 published external paper to present.

## Why this exists

GrimbaNews's methodology + open-source positioning needs a face in journalism+academic conference rooms. Without participation, the program is invisible to the people most likely to evaluate, adopt, and cite it.

## v1 target conferences

| Conference | Cadence | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Médias en Seine (Paris) | annual | French journalism flagship; Grimba-FR audience |
| ISOJ (Austin) | annual | International symposium on online journalism; academic reach |
| Online News Association (ONA) | annual | US digital-news practitioners |
| Computation+Journalism Symposium | annual | Methodology-aligned audience |
| Mozilla Festival | annual | OSS + ethics-aligned crowd |
| JournalismAI Festival | annual | AI in news with editorial focus |

## v1 commitment

- 1 talk submitted per conference per year (acceptance not guaranteed).
- 1 attendee min per event (Lucy, Henry, or designated speaker).
- Public slides + paper companion published on the methodology page within 30 days post-event.

## Anti-patterns

- No sponsor-funded keynotes that compromise editorial neutrality.
- No "thought-leadership" speaking gigs without methodological substance.
- No travel-without-deliverable.
