**Status:** plan v0

**Gating dependency:** Sports editor + per-discipline source roster.

## Why this exists

Football dominates GrimbaNews sports coverage today. International sports (rugby, basketball, athletics, cycling, tennis, esports, Olympic disciplines) underserve regional readers.

## Per-discipline rosters

- **Rugby:** Le Rugbynistère, RugbyPass, RugbyWorld, Midi Olympique.
- **Basketball:** BeBasket, Bleacher Report (US), ESPN NBA section.
- **Athletics:** L'Équipe athletics, Athletisme.fr, Athletics Weekly.
- **Cycling:** Cyclingnews, Vélo Magazine, Procyclingstats.
- **Tennis:** Tennis Magazine, Tennis World Tour.
- **Esports:** O'Gaming, ESPN Esports, Liquipedia.
- **Olympic sports per cycle:** rotating sport-of-the-year focus.

## Cadence

Per-discipline: weekly editorial cluster + per-event spike coverage (e.g. Roland Garros, Tour de France, NBA Finals).
