# GrimbaNews — Risk Register Quarterly Cadence

**Status:** plan v0
**Owner:** Sara Chen (CISO) + Vader + per-control owners
**Walks:** Mythos S1838 (risk-register cadence — quarterly review) deferred → partial
**Gating dependency:** S1837 conformant risk register version.

## Per-quarter review agenda

1. New risks identified in past quarter.
2. Per-existing-risk re-score (Inherent + Residual).
3. Per-control-effectiveness updates.
4. Per-treatment-decision changes.
5. Per-incident impact on risk register.
6. Per-quarter ranking changes.

## Per-attendee responsibilities

- Sara: lead facilitator + per-risk consolidation.
- Vader: per-Critical/High residual sign-off.
- Per-control owner: per-control status update.
- Per-domain lead: per-domain new-risk identification.

## Per-emergency review

Per-incident with material risk impact: ad-hoc register update within 7 days.

## Per-year roll-up

End-of-year: full risk register published in operator-archive + summarized in transparency report (per Wave LLL `docs/GRIMBANEWS_TRANSPARENCY_REPORT_SCOPE.md`).

## Per-quarter sign-off

Sara + Vader sign per-quarter risk register snapshot. Archived in ISMS evidence vault per Wave SUB-35.
