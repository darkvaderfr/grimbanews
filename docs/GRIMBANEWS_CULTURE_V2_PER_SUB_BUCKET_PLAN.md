## Why this exists

Culture is one of the highest-engagement reader topics. Today flat bucket conflates books vs film vs music vs theater. Per-sub-bucket lets readers follow their specific interest.

## Sub-buckets

- **24. Arts visuels** — peinture, sculpture, photographie, exhibitions, art market.
- **25. Spectacle vivant** — théâtre, danse, opéra, festivals.
- **26. Musique** — albums, tours, industry.
- **27. Cinéma + audiovisuel** — film, TV, streaming.

Plus extensions:
- **24a. Livres** — fiction, nonfiction, essais, edition.
- **24b. Jeux vidéo** — releases, industry, esports.
- **24c. Mode + design** — collections, designers, trends.

## Editorial cadence

Per-sub-bucket editor (could overlap; e.g. one editor covers books + theater):
- Daily 1-article minimum
- Weekly per-sub-bucket newsletter
- Per-event coverage (Cannes, Festival d'Avignon, Nobel literature, etc.)
